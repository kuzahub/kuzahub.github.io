Why Agribusiness is the CURRENT BIG THING

Food demand in Africa is set to grow by 60% by 2030. Africa holds 60% of the world’s arable land. It is estimated that Africa can feed the whole world.

Food Market Industry in Africa to hit
One Trillion Dollars

“Africa’s farmers and agribusinesses could create a trillion-dollar food market by 2030 if they can expand their access to more capital, electricity, better technology and irrigated land to grow high-value nutritious foods.”

The World Bank

Demand for mushrooms in Kenya at 1200 tons, only 500 is produced

“Kenya produces 500 tons per annum against an annual demand of 1200 tons both in hotels and home consumption. Mushrooms have high returns, are highly nutritious and low in cholestral.”

National Farmers Information System

Agriculture, a driver to Kenya's 10% Economic Growth

“Agriculture sector in Kenya contributes over 75% of industrial raw materials and more than 50% of the export earnings.”

Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Organization

Why Join Us

We understand that farming is not that easy

Food Security

With a fast growing African population, we must strengthen our food security to meet the rising demand. Our goal is to teach the best farming practices to increase food production.

Youth Empowerment

Youth unemployment in Africa is the highest in the whole world. Agriculture has the potential to bridge this gap while contributing to economic development. Our mission is to equip the youth, work with them and to empower them to be self-employed.

Technology

The use of technology on and off the farm can transform the agriculture sector in Africa. We focus on new ways of doing sustainable farming, with the help of technology.
